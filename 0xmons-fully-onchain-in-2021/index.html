0xmons Fully Onchain In 2021

Only 87 0xmons had their GAN animations put onchain in 2021, making them the first known onchain AI artworks.
While some other 0xmons had still images of their artwork put onchain, this list focuses only on those that had their full artwork (animation) put onchain.
See 0xmons v2 Cthulhu: On-chain Encoding for more info.
Note: The first AI artwork to include both AI art AND the algo/neural network on chain is XOR by Nahiko, which was releaased in 9th August 2021.
With that in mind, 0xmons with onchain animations released before this date could therefore be of extra interest.
